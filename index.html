  What is LinkedIn?

LinkedIn is the world's largest professional networking platform, used by 900 million+ professionals globally. Think of it as your digital CV, networking hub and industry knowledge feed all in one place.

  • Share career updates, skills, and milestones with your network
  • Connect with recruiters, hiring managers, and industry peers
  • Discover job openings and showcase your professional brand
  • Learn from thought leaders and industry conversations

Why does writing quality matter? Every post you publish is visible to potential employers, clients and colleagues. A single grammar error can undermine an otherwise impressive update, which is exactly what this course addresses.

  Before We Start

AI Has Limits

While ChatGPT can spot grammar mistakes, it's not perfect. It might miss errors or suggest unnatural changes. Always double-check corrections and ask a human for a second opinion, especially for important texts.

Human judgement is key.
Use AI as a starting point, not the final word. Your own reading of the corrected text always matters most.

Copy this prompt, paste it into ChatGPT, then add Alex's post after it. Asking ChatGPT to name the error type and explain why turns every correction into a mini grammar lesson, so you stop making the same mistake twice.

// ChatGPT prompt 
Please proofread the following LinkedIn post.
Fix any grammar errors and improve clarity.
For each correction, please:
1. Quote the original error
2. Show the corrected version
3. Name the error type (e.g. missing article,
   wrong verb tense, incorrect plural)
4. Explain why it's incorrect

Keep my original tone and voice. Only fix
errors, do not rewrite the entire post.
